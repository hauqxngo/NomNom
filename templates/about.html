About

Hi, there. Welcome to Nom Nom!

My name is Hau. I created this app in order to help cooking and planning meals easier as it appears in a form of a to-do list, instead, you have a to-cook list.

After having a baby, and the little one grows into a toddler who needs her food to be varied, I have the need to plan things out every single day.

I hope you found this helpful. If you have any feedback, please reach out at hauqxngo@gmail.com.

How to use this app:

It is quite simple and explanatory.
For non-members:
  • Get a random recipe filtered by your diets or restrictions (ex: vegan, keto, gluten-free, dairy-free, etc.).
    • For registered users (join for free), you can:
    • Add a random recipe to your to-cook list ✓
    • Add your own recipe ✓
    • Add a recipe link you stumbled accross somewhere (ex: videos from YouTube, Instagram, Tiktok, etc) ✓
    • Check your recipe off the list after you made one ✓
    Thanks for visiting and time to get cooking!
