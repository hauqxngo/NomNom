About

Hi, there!

My name is Hau. I created this app in order to help cooking and planning meals easier as it appears in a form of a to-do list, instead, you have a to-cook list.

After having a baby and the little one grows into a toddler who needs her food to be varied. I've actually been doing this manually by writing on a board on my fridge every time I'm planning/thinking about what I want to cook next. Also, the purpose is to avoid spoiled fresh vegetables or leftovers that could be forgotten in the back of the fridge. However, I didn't have the luxury of getting a random recipe to add to my list like this.

I hope you found this helpful. If you have any feedback, please reach out at hauqxngo@gmail.com. I appreciate your input.

Thanks for visiting and have a great day!
