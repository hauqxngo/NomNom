About

Hi, there!

My name is Hau. I created this app in order to help cooking and planning meals easier as it appears in a form of a to-do list, instead, you have a to-cook list.

After having a baby, and the little one grows into a toddler who needs her food to be varied, I have the need to plan things out every single day. I was actually doing this manually by writing on a board on my fridge every time I'm thinking about what I want to cook next.

I hope you found this helpful. If you have any feedback, please reach out at hauqxngo@gmail.com.

Thanks for visiting and have a great day!
